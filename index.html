Elemental Magic in Fantasy Worlds

A basic introduction into the portrayal of element based magic in fantasy worldbuilding.

Alchemy Elements

Fire is the element of intesity. Magic spells in the fire group have high spell intensity, damage radius and speed.
Water is the element of control. Magic spells in the water group have good spell handling, healing, precision and redirection.
Earth is the element of constituation. Magic spells in the earth group have good defensive properties, are hard to block and have longer-term impact.
Air is the element of intesity. Alchemy ingredients in the air group have high mobility, flexibility and space control.
